## Tips for Creating Your Own

1. **Background Colors**: Use dark colors for better battery life on OLED screens
2. **Passed Color**: Should be vibrant and stand out
3. **Future Color**: Should be subtle, darker than background
4. **Text Color**: Should contrast well with background
5. **Padding**: Add padding for notches/punch holes on modern phones
6. **Columns**: 
   - Month mode: 10-16 columns work well
   - Year mode: 20-30 columns recommended
